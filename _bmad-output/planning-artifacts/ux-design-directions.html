Rules: Daemon Prince Abilities

This unit has the DAEMON and KHORNE keywords. It can fly. At the start of your hero phase, if this model is within 8" of any enemy models, you can roll a dice. On a 4+, the closest enemy unit suffers D3 mortal wounds.
